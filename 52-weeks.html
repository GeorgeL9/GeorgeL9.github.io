52 Week App Challenge

Build and Publish a New iOS App Every Week

Rationale

  • Improve my design and product skills
  • Become more familiar with Reach Native
  • Become a better time estimator, and force myself to avoid feature/scope creep
  • A good break from building things for others
  • Ship something, instead of having project ADHD

Rules / Schedule

Day Goals Time Limit
Sunday Create project concept, wireframes, graphic assets 4 Hours
Monday - Friday Development 2 Hours/ Day, 10 Hours Total
Saturday Create Marketing/Metadata, Submit to App Store 2 Hours
  • Project must be submitted on Saturday
  • After Sunday planning, features can only be cut, not added
  • Have fun, if this encourages my burn-out, take a few weeks off
  • Do not create apps with financial motivation first
